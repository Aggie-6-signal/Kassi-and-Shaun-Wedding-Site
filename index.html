Our Story

Every love story is beautiful, but ours is our favorite.

Can you believe it all started online!?

For Kassi, she was drawn to his smile. For Shaun, it was her eyes.

After long nights of engaging conversations and laughter, it didn't take long to realize that they shared the same goals and values. They went on their first date and have been together ever since.

After 3 years in love, Shaun asked Kassi for her hand in marriage in Jamaica on Valentines's Day of 2026. They are both very excited about their union, and look forward to sharing it with their close family and friends.
